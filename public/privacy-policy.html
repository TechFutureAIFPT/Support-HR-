Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Support HR, operated by TechFuture, accesses, uses, stores, protects, and shares information when users use our AI recruiting platform.

1. App Purpose

Support HR helps recruiting teams upload job descriptions, import candidate files, screen CVs, compare candidates against hiring criteria, generate interview questions, and review AI-assisted recruiting insights. The app uses Google sign-in and, when enabled by the user, Google Drive access to let users select files for recruiting workflows.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Account information such as name, email address, profile image, authentication provider, and user ID.
  • Recruiting content such as job descriptions, screening criteria, CVs, uploaded files, extracted text, and analysis results.
  • Product usage data such as workflow state, saved sessions, feedback, and basic technical logs.
  • Google user data described in the next section when a user chooses to sign in with Google or connect Google Drive.

3. Google User Data

When users authenticate with Google, Support HR may access basic Google account information such as the user's name, email address, profile photo, and Google account identifier. When users connect Google Drive, Support HR requests read-only access so the user can browse and select files to import.

For Google Drive imports, Support HR may process:

  • File metadata needed for selection, such as file name, file ID, MIME type, file size, and modified time.
  • The contents of files that the user explicitly selects for import into Support HR.
  • Extracted text and analysis results generated from selected files for CV screening or JD analysis.

Support HR does not access all Drive files for unrelated purposes. The read-only Google Drive scope is used only to let the user select recruiting documents and import the selected files into the app.

4. How We Use Information

  • To create and secure user accounts.
  • To import selected CV or job-description files from Google Drive.
  • To extract text, score candidates, compare candidates with job criteria, and generate recruiting insights.
  • To save user workflows, history, templates, and feedback when the user chooses to use those features.
  • To operate, debug, secure, and improve the Support HR service.

5. Sharing and Transfer

We do not sell Google user data or personal information. We may share information only with service providers that help us operate the product, such as hosting, authentication, database, storage, backend infrastructure, and AI processing providers. These providers are used only to provide, secure, maintain, and improve Support HR functionality.

Support HR's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Retention and Deletion

We retain account information and recruiting content for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain user workflows, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Users may request deletion of account data, uploaded files, imported Google Drive content, and related analysis results by contacting us. We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including HTTPS/TLS in transit, access controls, authentication, and server-side handling of sensitive credentials where applicable. No online service is completely risk-free, but we work to protect user data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

8. User Choices and Rights

Users may choose not to connect Google Drive. Users can disconnect access through their Google Account permissions page and may contact us to access, correct, export, or delete their data where applicable.

9. Changes

We may update this policy when our data practices change. If material changes are made, we will update the effective date and provide reasonable notice through the product or other appropriate channels.

10. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact Support HR at support@supporthr.vn.
